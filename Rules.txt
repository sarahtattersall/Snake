THE RULES:
GENERAL:
 - Snake moves in direction pressed until another keypress is received.
 - Collect food to make your snake grow
 - +n points per piece of food
 - only one piece of food on the board at the time (although with 2 players could have player food)
 - 
TO LOSE:
 - hit the wall (if you cannot travel through them)
 - hit yourself
 - hit another player 

TO WIN:
 - goes on forever until you become too big and loose.
